Aperol Spritz

Ingredients:

  • Ice
  • 100ml Aperol
  • 150ml prosecco
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • soda, to top up

Steps:

1. Put a couple of cubes of ice into 2 glasses and add a 50 ml measure of Aperol to each. Divide the prosecco between the glasses and then top up with soda, if you like.
